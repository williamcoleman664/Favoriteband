The Eden Project

Music Style

The Eden Project, which is Jonathon Ng, is classified as an electronic producer making atmospheric indie electronic music. Ng had an interview with Status and said

"Honestly I don't really mind how people lable my music and if they can't put one name on it, maybe that's a good thing."

I personally like not only the electronic style his produces, but also his voice.
